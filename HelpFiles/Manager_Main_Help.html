Manager Menu Navigation

Table of Contents

Click the button for the task you'd like to accomplish

To adjust inventory numbers and/or pricings click 'Update Inventory'

To add a new employee/Manager, click 'Add Employee'

To print sales reports click 'Print Reports'

To adjust tax rate or shipping rates click 'Price Settings'

To create, activate, or deactivate discount codes, click 'Manage Shop Codes'

To manage all current users, Employees, Managers, and Customers, click 'Manage Users'

To exit and return to the main menu click 'Return'
