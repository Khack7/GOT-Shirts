Employee Menu Navigation

Table of Contents

Click the button for the task you'd like to accomplish

To view or edit your personal information, click 'View Emp Info'. *Please note that some information can only be changed by the Manager*

To view all discount codes, click 'View Active Codes'. This also shows what codes are currently unavailable

To complete a purchase on behalf of a customer, click 'Customer Purchase'

To view inventory count and prices, click 'View Inventory'

To exit and return to the main menu click 'Return'
