How to shop for your new apparel

Table of Contents

To begin please select the color of shirt you want followed by the size at the bottom.

Additional colors can be chosen with the dropdown above the colored buttons

After a color and size are selected, enter the quantity you'd like to purchase then click 'Add to cart'

To remove an item from your cart, select the item then click 'Remove Selected'

If you have a coupon code, click 'Have a code?' and enter the code into the popup

If you would like to view your account information or if there is anything about your acccount information that needs to be changed, please click the Account button

Once an item is added to your cart you may procceed to checkout, otherwise if you'd like to exit and cancel your order click 'Cancel Order' to return to the main menu
